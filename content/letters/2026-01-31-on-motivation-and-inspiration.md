---
title: "On Motivation and Inspiration"
date: 2026-01-31T00:00:00+00:00
---

Don't believe the lie that these come to you from some external force. You will
never find them.

Motivation and inspiration are generated internally when you take action. When
you start doing, they kick in, which drives you to do more, and the cycle
continues.

If you want to accomplish something, don't wait for some mystical muse. Take
action and the rest will follow.
