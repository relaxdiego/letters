---
title: "It's OK to be Humbled"
date: 2026-02-20T00:00:00+00:00
---

It’s OK to be humbled every now and then.

It’s not always easy to accept because we’re so used to being the main character
in our head. But it’s an important experience even though it feels off and you
want to forget about it as soon as possible.

Don’t shake it off. Don’t dismiss it. Internalize it and let it guide you to
recognize and to learn from those who are better than you.
