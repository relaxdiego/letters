---
title: "Laugh with your body"
date: 2007-05-05T15:18:46+00:00
---

One of my little pleasures in life is when I find something (a joke, an unexpected event, etc.) so funny that I laugh with my entire body. In these instances, I feel that just laughing with my mouth is simply not enough to express how much I enjoy the moment. In these instances, I might briefly clap, lightly stomp my feet, tilt my head backwards, sometimes I even jump. This probably sounds strange to you. But trust me, son. You can’t truly enjoy a funny moment unless you laugh with your body.
