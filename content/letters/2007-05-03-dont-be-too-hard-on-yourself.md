---
title: "Don’t be too hard on yourself"
date: 2007-05-03T15:17:57+00:00
---

This has been one of my major weaknesses when I was younger. Whenever I make a
mistake, I berate myself so much that I end up discouraged from trying again.
Don’t make that same mistake, son. Nobody is perfect. Realize that you can’t
always get things right the first time. However, don’t let that be an excuse to
do sloppy work either. Always strive to excel, but if you fail to achieve your
target, find out why you failed, learn from it, then try again when you're
ready.
