---
title: "Remember to support the three I’s"
date: 2007-06-01T04:16:10+00:00
---

The [three I’s](../2007-05-28-remember-the-three-is/) that I previously mentioned are great. But also remember to support them with the following:

- **Love** – To constantly be there for those around you.
- **Happiness** – The habit of being sincerely appreciative of the many blessings in life.
- **Health** – Soundness of body and mind.
