---
title: "Life is full of challenges. Get over it."
date: 2007-05-15T02:16:37+00:00
---

Life will always present you with challenges no matter where you are and no matter what age you are in. Don’t let that bring you down, though. It’s actually a good thing. Think about how boring it would be if there were no challenges. We’d all probably just be sitting on the ground staring into space, scratching our heads, not knowing what to do. Having said all of that, try to find ways to cope. Learn to appreciate the little pleasures in life. Learn to laugh at your mistakes. [Laugh with your body](../2007-05-05-laugh-with-your-body/). [Value your relationships](../2007-05-07-the-source-of-true-happiness/). And, of course, know that your mother and I are always here for you when you need to rant.
