# Permission to reproduce these letters

I am Mark Maglana. I wrote every letter in this repository.

I grant to my wife, my children, my direct relatives, and all of my
descendants, for all time, the unrestricted and perpetual right to reproduce,
copy, print, bind, publish, distribute, translate, adapt, or perform these
letters, in whole or in part, in any form and by any means now known or later
invented, anywhere in the world, without payment to me or to my estate, and
without needing anyone's further permission.

This permission cannot be revoked. It passes to each new generation of my
descendants automatically.

A few of the letters quote other people — a song lyric, a passage from a book,
a line from a poem — and say plainly whose words they are. Those quotations
belong to their own authors, not to me, and I grant no rights over them.
Everything else in these letters is mine.

I am writing this down so that no printer, publisher, bookbinder, archive, or
website ever has cause to refuse my family a copy of my own words.

Mark Maglana
9 July 2026
